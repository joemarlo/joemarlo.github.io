About me, Joe Marlo

I work as a data scientist at Verizon focused on forecasting. I recently graduated from New York University with a master’s in applied statistics. My advisor was Dr. Marc Scott and my continuing research is on sequence analysis (categorical time-series data). I’m also a current volunteer and previous graduate research assistant on thinkCausal, a PRIISM project to build scaffolded causal inference software implementing Bayesian Additive Regression Trees for applied researchers.

Previously, I’ve worked under Dr. Jennifer Hill, interned at Verizon in a data science role, and worked at J.P. Morgan. The majority of my latter role focused on using proprietary big data and public surveys to study the financial challenges older Americans face. I later switched roles to build a new client-facing investing platform and robo-advisor designed to serve all Americans.

Please reach out on LinkedIn if you have any questions or comments.

The domain marlo.works is – in addition to meaning “production” or a “studio” – a reference to John Cooper Works.
