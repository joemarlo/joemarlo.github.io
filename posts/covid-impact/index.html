Enumerating the economic and social shifts of March 2020

Work-in-progress | The ex-health impact of COVID-19

I’m not an epidemiologist so I’m abstaining from building COVID prediction models. In lieu, I’m spending my isolation time tracking down various economic and social data illustrating the vast shifts and adjustments in our lives recently.
