Enumerating the economic shifts of March 2020

Work-in-progress | the impact of COVID-19 on transportation and the economy

This is a working page of plots highlighting the non-health related impact of COVID-19. Some of the interactive d3.js has small bugs. All plots are drafts.

Daily NYC subway ridership drops significantly in March and Citibike is expected to tick up


Data from MTA turnstiles and Citibike
2019 data has been shifted two days to match 2020 weekdays
March Citibike data not yet available

Weekly unemployment claims have skyrocketed


Data from St. Louis Federal Reserve

Daily worldwide flights have plummeted


Data from flightradar24.com
Includes passenger, cargo, charter, and some business jet flights

Daily change in mobile phone location: Retail & recreation

Data from Google Mobility Reports. United States.
